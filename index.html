About me

Welcome to my portfolio!


I'm Alina, a passionate Web Developer specializing in frontend development. My journey in the tech industry began early, driven by a fascination with how technology shapes our world. I initially started my career in IT Sales, focusing on the data center and managed IT services sectors. This experience provided me with a deep understanding of how businesses utilize technology to solve problems and improve operations, as well as valuable insights into the broader tech industry from a service and sales perspective.


Over time, I discovered my true passion for frontend development, where creativity and technology intersect. Now, I am fully committed to building engaging, responsive, and user-friendly web applications. I have been honing my skills through self-study, courses, and personal projects, gaining proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like Vue.js.


Frontend development excites me because it allows me to create visually appealing and intuitive user experiences. Each new tool or technology I encounter challenges me to think critically and continuously improve, which makes this field incredibly rewarding. I am excited to take on new projects where I can apply my growing knowledge and continue developing my expertise in frontend development.
